NPCs You Will Interact With:

    The Prime Minister's Request

    Level Requirement: 140 - 160

    Start: Prime Minister Erbe (Mondshine)

    Objective: Meet Border Guard Boru.

    End: Boundary Guard Boru (Mondshine)

    Reward: Experience: 530,000,000

    Lightning Assault

    Level Requirement: 140 - 160

    Start: Boundary Guard Boru (Mondshine)

    Objective: Kill all Heretics (Soldier, Chief, and Watchdog) at the Eastern Guard Post.

    End: Boundary Guard Boru (Mondshine)

    Reward: Experience: 4,500,000,000 | Skill Points: 10

    Note: Eastern Guard Post is actually in the north of Mondshine - see 'tents' on the map near town.

    Put to the Torch

    Level Requirement: 140 - 160

    Start: Boundary Guard Boru (Mondshine)

    Objective: Burn the Heretic's Camps. Get Torches from a vendor in the village.

    End: Boundary Guard Boru (Mondshine)

    Reward: Experience: 3,000,000,000 | Skill Points: 10

    Note: Buy three torches from Grocer Mary: Torch of Commander, Torch of Guard and Torch of Guard Post. Use them in the southeastern Guard Post at coordinates (1739,2110), (1800,2208), (1864,2098).
